About

I build with whatever the future just shipped.

What pulls me now is how much one curious person can make with AI in the loop. I vibe-code — describe what I want, then shape it with the machine until it's real — and I use it to build the tools and toys I wished existed.

I moved from India to Copenhagen in 2022 and I'm learning Danish in the open. I treat every shift in the tech as an invitation, not a threat — this site is where I share what I'm building as I go.
