About

Clarity from complexity — that's the whole job.

I lead sales-performance and data-visibility work at a global logistics company, turning complex commercial data into things people can actually decide on. I moved from India to Copenhagen in 2022 and have been learning Danish with an aim to clear PD3 soon.

This site is where I share what I build — starting with the resources and tools I wished existed when I began.
