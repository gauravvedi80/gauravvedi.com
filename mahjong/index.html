How to play

Goal — clear the whole board by removing matching pairs of tiles.

Free tiles — a tile can be picked only when no tile rests on top of it and at least one of its left or right sides is open. Locked tiles dim slightly when tapped.

Matching — tiles match when they show the same face. Any flower matches any flower; any season matches any season.

Hint 💡 — highlights a valid pair. Tap again to cycle through all available pairs.

Shuffle 🔀 — re-deals the remaining tiles into a board that is always solvable from that point.

Undo ↩ — takes back any number of moves, even after a shuffle.

Daily Challenge — everyone gets the same board each day. Win it to extend your daily streak.

Every board is solvable — boards are dealt by reversing a legal removal sequence, so a path to victory always exists from the start.
