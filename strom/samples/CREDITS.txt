Strøm — audio samples
=====================

Every sample in this folder is public domain (CC0 1.0 or an explicit PD
dedication) — no attribution required. Provenance is recorded here anyway, as
proof of clean licensing.

  Source: Meadowlark Factory Library — "Kickmess Snappy 3"
  https://github.com/BillyDM/meadowlark-factory-library  (CC0 1.0)

clap.wav, hat.wav, snare.wav, perc.wav
  Source: Versilian Community Sample Library (VCSL)
  - clap.wav  : Idiophones/Struck Idiophones/Claps/Clap_rr1
  - hat.wav   : Idiophones/Struck Idiophones/Hi-Hat Cymbal/HiHat_Close_rr1_Mid
  - snare.wav : Membranophones/.../Legacy Snares/OldSnare/snare_mf1
  - perc.wav  : Membranophones/.../Bongos/BongoH_Hit1_v1_rr1_Mid
  https://github.com/sgossner/VCSL  (CC0 1.0)

cheer.wav
  Source: Wikimedia Commons — "Clapping hurray (cropped).oga" by starlite
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Clapping_hurray_(cropped).oga
  License: Public Domain — "This work has been released into the public domain
  by its author, starlite." (cropped to a ~2.4s hit for the Cheer pad)

Processing: converted to mono 32 kHz 16-bit, leading silence trimmed, peak-
normalized, lengths capped. No other content was used.
